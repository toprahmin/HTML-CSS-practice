Intro

After reading through this site you will have learned what it was really like to have grown up in these streets. I would like to add that experiences encountered during this journey are not to be repeated unless your goal is to be where I am today. Repeat at your own risk.

Throughout the journey these streets have taught me a lot. Such as, Pat's and Geno's only get customers who dont live in Philly. Also, dont just go around picking up any dollar bill you see on the ground. Chances are it may be a poop Dollar. Another tip, turn signals are just that, signals. They aren't used for asking someone permission to enter their lane. You tell them you are coming into their lane.
